Sierra Leone Civil and Voting Registry

The company has successfully implemented, delivered and trained NCRA staff in the usage of biometric registration and biometric voting systems. We provided NCRA with the best technology to identify and authenticate individuals security based on unique criteriaâ€™s such as fingerprints, decentralized hubs for registrations, and redundant infrastructures. Indeed, SMRT has been awarded the contract for the supply, support and train of biometric kits to the NCRA through a bid dated back in 2016. SMRT which acted as the local contractor engaged into this project with a strategic alliance with Smartmatic, a company specialized in this field based in Amsterdam in the Netherlands. In order to cover the whole country and the entire population, we were to divide the country into 14 districts including Freetown as a one district. Over 5 million unique citizens were registered throughout the country for the purpose of elections. SMRT and Smartmatic took in charge the training of local public staff, and in addition SMRT continued the support of the project for an extra year after accomplishing the terms of the whole project by building a NSC (National Support Center) to address any hardware or software issues and train additional employees.
